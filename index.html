Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

A kindergarten (3-6 years) student is in a crucial stage of early learning, developing social, emotional, cognitive, and motor skills through play-based activities. They explore numbers, letters, creativity, and problem-solving in a nurturing and interactive environment.

Read More

Elementary School

An elementary school (6-12 years) student builds foundational skills in reading, writing, math, and critical thinking while developing social and emotional growth. They engage in structured learning, teamwork, and problem-solving to prepare for more advanced education.

Read More

Middle (10-16) Years

A middle school (10-16 years) student focuses on developing deeper academic skills in subjects like math, science, and language arts. They also refine their social abilities, explore extracurricular interests, and gain a sense of independence in their learning journey.
